Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data when using this platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as username, email address, and profile details
  • User-generated content including posts, comments, and messages
  • Usage data such as pages visited, interactions, and feature usage
  • Device information including IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Log data for security, debugging, and abuse prevention

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the platform
  • To improve performance, features, and user experience
  • To detect, prevent, and address abuse, spam, and security threats
  • To enforce community rules and moderation decisions

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information only when required to comply with legal obligations, enforce our policies, or protect the safety and integrity of the platform.

4. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our policies. Deleted content may remain in backups for a limited period.

5. User Rights

  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data
  • You may request access to your stored personal data where applicable
  • You may update or correct your account information at any time

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access.
