Accessibility Statement

We are committed to improving accessibility and ensuring the platform is usable by as many people as possible, regardless of ability or device.

1. Our Commitment

We aim to conform to recognized accessibility standards such as WCAG 2.1 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) at the AA level where reasonably possible.

2. Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Keyboard navigation support for core interactions
  • Responsive design for mobile and low-resolution devices
  • Readable contrast and scalable text support

3. Known Limitations

Because this platform relies on user-generated content, we cannot guarantee that all content posted by users will meet accessibility standards (such as alt text for images or structured formatting).

4. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We continuously improve interface components, navigation, and compatibility based on user feedback and technical reviews.

5. Reporting Accessibility Issues

If you encounter accessibility barriers while using the platform, please contact us with details so we can investigate and improve the experience.
