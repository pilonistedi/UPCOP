Platform Rules

These rules apply to all users and content on the platform. They are enforced to maintain safety, trust, and order within the community.

1. Illegal Content

  • No content that promotes, facilitates, or depicts illegal activity
  • No instructions for wrongdoing or harm
  • No distribution of stolen or unauthorized material

2. Harassment & Abuse

  • No targeted harassment, bullying, or intimidation
  • No hate speech or demeaning content based on identity or characteristics
  • No repeated unwanted contact or stalking behavior

3. Spam & Manipulation

  • No spam, repetitive posting, or low-effort content flooding
  • No vote manipulation or artificial engagement
  • No bot abuse or automated exploitation without permission

4. Account Integrity

  • You are responsible for all activity under your account
  • No impersonation of individuals, brands, or organizations
  • No sharing or selling of accounts

5. Privacy Violations

  • No posting of private personal data without consent
  • No doxxing or attempts to expose private identities
  • No sharing of sensitive information belonging to others

6. Enforcement

Violations may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination depending on severity and recurrence. Enforcement decisions are made at the platform’s discretion.
